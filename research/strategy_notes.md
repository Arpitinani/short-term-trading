# Strategy Research Notes

## Successful Traders & Their Methods

### Mark Minervini (2x US Investing Champion) — SEPA / VCP
- **Volatility Contraction Pattern (VCP):** Stock in Stage 2 uptrend consolidates in progressively tighter contractions (e.g., 25% → 15% → 8% → 3%). Volume dries up at the pivot. Buy breakout on volume surge (50%+ above average).
- **Trend Template (ALL must be true):**
  1. Price > 150-day MA AND 200-day MA
  2. 150-day MA > 200-day MA
  3. 200-day MA rising for 1+ month (ideally 4-5 months)
  4. 50-day MA > 150-day AND 200-day
  5. Price > 50-day MA
  6. Price at least 25% above 52-week low (ideally 100%+)
  7. Price within 25% of 52-week high (closer = better)
  8. Relative Strength ranking 70+ (ideally 90+)
- Risk: Max 7-8% loss, often tighter 3-5% using VCP low. Risks 1-1.25% of portfolio per trade. 3:1 R minimum.

### William O'Neil — CAN SLIM
- C: Current quarterly earnings 25%+ YoY
- A: Annual earnings 25%+ for 3-5 years
- N: New product/management/price high
- S: Supply & demand — low float, heavy volume on breakouts
- L: Leader in industry (RS 80+)
- I: Institutional sponsorship increasing
- M: Market in confirmed uptrend (follow-through day)
- Core pattern: Cup-and-handle. Buy at top of handle + $0.10. Volume 40-50% above avg.
- Sell at 20-25% profit. Cut ALL losses at 7-8%.

### Ross Cameron (Warrior Trading) — Gap and Go
- Small-cap momentum ($2-$20), gapping 10%+ premarket, RVOL > 2x, float < 20M, news catalyst
- Buy first pullback within first 30 min
- 2:1 minimum reward-to-risk, max daily loss = 3x average winner

### Linda Raschke — Short-term Swing
- "Holy Grail": ADX > 30, buy first pullback to 20 EMA
- "Turtle Soup": Fade false breakouts of 20-day highs/lows
- Uses 3/10/16 MACD oscillator
- Very tight stops, 50-55% win rate, 2:1+ reward-to-risk

### Kristjan Kullamägi — Momentum Breakouts
- Turned ~$5K into $80M+ (publicly shared, not independently audited)
- Influenced by Minervini and O'Neil
- Episodic pivots (earnings gaps, IPO breakouts), VCP breakouts
- Holds 2-20 days, concentrates in 3-5 positions max
- Risks 0.5-1% per trade, up to 2-3% on highest conviction

## Key Concepts

### What Has Statistical Edge (backed by research/track records)
1. **Trend + Pullback + Volume** (Minervini's SEPA/VCP) — most documented swing edge
2. **RSI(2) Mean Reversion** on uptrending stocks (Connors, 75%+ win rate)
3. **Momentum + Relative Strength** — academic research confirms (Jegadeesh & Titman, 1993)
4. **Opening Range Breakout** with trend filter (Crabel, 1990)
5. **Volume precedes price** — dry-up at support → surge on breakout

### What Does NOT Have Reliable Edge
- Indicator-only strategies (pure RSI overbought/oversold, MACD crossover alone)
- Candlestick patterns in isolation without context
- Any single-indicator approach without volume and trend context

## Critical Reality Check
- 97% of persistent day traders lose money (Barber et al., 2020)
- Swing trading (2-20 day holds) has better probability for part-time traders
- Consistent profitability typically takes 6-12+ months
- Paper trading doesn't replicate the psychology of real money
